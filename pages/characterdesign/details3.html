VIONETE

Vionete is a representation of a woman from Suka Dayak. This is depicted through her clothing which is traditional Dayak clothing. She is a fictional character of a princess who has the power of the wind. The wind itself is a representation of the mystical form that exists in the Dayak tribe, namely witchcraft. Santet itself is a deadly but invisible science. Like the wind, if it has a large frequency with a certain speed, it will be able to hurt and kill even though it cannot be seen directly by the eye.
